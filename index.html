Start-up Ideas

Start-up Ideas is a web project that I implemented a couple of weeks ago. It allows users to submit ideas about “Start-up” companies. A start-up idea includes information about the title of the idea, a short description, the industry or market it belongs to and a set of relevant keywords/tags that better describe it. a registered user can navigate the list of already registered “start-up” ideas and filter ideas by industry/market, by keyword/tag or sort them by name or date of submission. Also, a registered user can express her preference to an idea by liking or disliking it.
